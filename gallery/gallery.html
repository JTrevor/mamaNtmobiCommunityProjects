Pre-School Programme

When we first started the little children aged from 3-5 years could not even hold a pencil. Now they are drawing and following lines. This gives the child a head start in life when they go to "Big" School.

Pre-School Programme

There are currently no preschool at Ezinketheni so MNCP is filling in the gap by holding a programme for the children on Tuesdays and Thursday.

Breakfast Club

300 children are provided with a nutritious porridge sponsored by JAM before they go to school. These children would otherwise be going to school on an empty stomach.

Workplace Integrated Learning / Internship

Student Auxiliary Social Workers spend three months with MNCP gaining in the field experience. Every three months we receive a new set of students.
